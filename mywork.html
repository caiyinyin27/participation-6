Comic Strips

第一話：家姐們的早晨大戰

A busy breakfast scene where siblings argue over food, parents react in shock, and the calm second sister quietly takes control in front of the whole family at home there.

第二話：爺爺的秘密招數

A granddaughter complains to her grandpa, who secretly teaches her a clever plan. She prepares snacks and a small note, and her father finally softens and praises her warmly, gently.

第三話：細佬的求救信號

The younger brother is forced into a pink dress, begging the second sister to save him. She negotiates, then helps him escape while everyone is distracted and laughing happily together.

第四話：嫲嫲的美食陷阱

Grandma bakes fresh egg tarts while the girl waits impatiently. They bargain playfully over washing dishes, and the younger sister happily enjoys the dessert as grandma smiles in satisfaction nearby.

第五話：爸爸的奇怪補償

The daughter confronts her busy father for ignoring her. He offers a teddy bear with a promise note, but she insists that true companionship cannot be replaced by material gifts.
